How It Works

Righty is a smart companion for anyone actively looking for a job. If you've ever found a job advert and wondered: “Am I the right fit for this?” — especially in terms of experience level or expected salary — then this tool is for you.

Instead of guessing or applying blindly, just copy the job advert text and paste it into Righty. Our machine learning model will analyze the advert and instantly give you insights about experince level and salary expectations.

This tool is not meant to replace your judgment — it’s here to support it. The goal is to help you make more confident decisions and avoid wasting time on roles that might not match your background or expectations.
